          About Us

          At Baffinland, we work and live beyond the Arctic Circle, mining iron ore at our Mary River property. Located in the Qikiqtani Region on Baffin Island, Nunavut, it’s one of the world’s most captivating and seldom seen destinations and we are looking for people ready to share, build, and grow with our company.

          We are a unique company with a strong management team. Our leadership team has amassed specialty experience on northern mining projects from community consultation and permitting, through to operation. Our workforce brings together the modern practice of safe, sustainable mining and the enduring value of a traditional culture, which is rapidly advancing. The result is a work and community experience you won't find anywhere else.

          Students and recent graduates are the future of Baffinland and of our country. Baffinland has created tailored internship programs for recent Inuit graduates and is currently recruiting interns to join our team on a 12-month internship.

          Main Accountabilities

          • A desire to start a career, and explore opportunities
          • Fulfill Task set out by supervisor in assigned department
          • Assist with day to day task within the department
          • Perform some clerical duties
          • Participate in regular monthly follow up calls to discuss internship program and opportunities
          • Conduct oneself in a professional and responsible manner

          Main Qualifications

          • Recent Graduate of Post-Secondary Institute
          • Eager to learn and work with a department within the organization
          • Good verbal and written communication skills
          • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite
          • Able to work under pressure and meet deadlines
          • Willingness to work at a remote mine site on a fly in / fly out basis
